About me

Meet Mohan Kulapathi— a Health and Wellness expert based in Bangalore, with over 20 years of experience in the fitness industry.

When I was cut from a baseball team in school for being too weak, I read every fitness book and magazine that I could get my hands on. I dedicated myself to a strict weightlifting routine and diet program. Funny thing is that by the time I was more than prepared for the team, I didn’t care about it anymore.

Transforming my own body made me realize what I really cared about was health, fitness, and helping people. I graduated a year early from university with my Bachelor of Science degree in Kinesiology. I had also earned my personal training certifications ahead of schedule. As I helped people achieve their fitness goals, I spent years honing my craft, mentoring with some of the most respected names in the industry, and attaining certifications in the following areas: NSCA Certified Personal Trainer NSCA Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist ACSM Certified Exercise Physiologist Precision Nutrition Coach.
